About

I am a Senior Data Engineer based in Montreal, Canada, with deep experience designing and operating data platforms that serve analytics, reporting, and machine learning at scale.

My work centers on cloud-native data architectures—data lakes, medallion-style layers, and distributed batch and streaming pipelines—on AWS and Databricks. I care about clean design, observability, and enabling data scientists and analysts with reliable, well-documented datasets.

I have led data engineering teams and contributed to platform strategy, data mesh adoption, and mentoring. Beyond production systems, I stay active in the community as an AWS Community Builder (Data), through user groups and technical talks, sharing practices on data engineering and cloud data services.
